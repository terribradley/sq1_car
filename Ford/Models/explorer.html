Test Drive the 2015 Explorer at Portland's top Ford Dealership

The Ford Explorer is an affordable and highly capable mid-size SUV. Equipped with advanced technology like Intelligent 4WD, the Explorer is built to get you where you need to go. And with three rows and seating for 7, the Explorer is bigger than it looks.

The standard 3.5L V6 engine delivers 290 horsepower, giving the Explorer a sporty feel and making it easy to tow upwards of 5,000lbs. Meanwhile the standard AdvanceTrac and Trailer Sway Control systems help ensure you have maximum control in even the most extreme road conditions.
