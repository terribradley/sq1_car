Test Drive the 2015 Expedition at Portland's top Ford Dealership

The Ford Expedition is a full-size SUV that's built to go the distance. With seating for 8 and a third-row row that folds flat to the floor, there's plenty of room for your crew and your cargo. Up front, a leather-wrapped steering wheel with audio controls, rear view camera, Ford SYNC system, and optional heated and cooled seats will keep you happy in the cockpit for hours.

The Expedition gets its power from 5.4L V8 engine. With 310 horsepower and 365 lb.-ft. of torque, the Expedition is able to pull up to 9,200lbs., making it the true do-it-all SUV. Come find yours at Damerow Ford in Portland.
