Test Drive the 2015 Expedition EL at Portland's top Ford Dealership

The Ford Expedition EL ups the ante with an extra-long chassis. Seat up to 8 and carry even more cargo, or fold the third row flat to the floor for maximum cargo space. Like its shorter sibling, the Expedition EL is powered by a 5.4L V8 engine, netting 310 horsepower and 365lb.-ft. of torque. That's enough raw power to haul up to 9,200lbs.

The Expedition EL also comes standard with a variety of luxury and convenience features, like a leather-wrapped steering wheel with audio controls, rear view backup camera, and the Ford SYNC system. A host of optional upgrades are also available to make the Expedition EL truly your own. Come find yours at Damerow Ford in Portland.
