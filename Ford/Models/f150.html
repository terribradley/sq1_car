If you are in the market for a full-size pickup truck, look no further than the Ford F-150. The F-150's highly capable chassis begins with a rugged, fully boxed frame as the foundation. This paves the way for its best-in-class hauling capabilities. The F-150 can haul 3,120 pounds - more than any of its competitors. And when the job requires even more power, the F-150's Class V trailer towing package lets you tow up to 11,300 pounds. Learn more about the F-150's tow capacity with Ford's towing guides.

The F-150 is a smart workhorse, featuring SRW (Single Rear Wheels) with integrated Trailer Brake Controller (TBC) technology that synchronizes your vehicle and trailer brakes to provide safety and confidence. On top of that, Trailer Sway Control, Electronic-Locking Rear Differential, and AdvanceTrac® with Roll Stability Control? (RSC®) technologies ensure a smooth and safe ride, no matter how big the job.
