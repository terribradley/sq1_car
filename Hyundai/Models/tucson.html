Test Drive the 2016 Tuscon at Portland's top Hyundai Dealership

The Hyundai Tucson is a spacious SUV that offers plenty of passenger and cargo room. Amenities include a panoramic sunroof, front solar glass, and heated side mirrors for when the cold, winter weather arrives. Inside, the Tucson includes 5 spacious seats and dual automatic temperature control to keep all your passengers happy and comfortable. High tech comes standard in the Tucson, with a rearview camera, touchscreen display, and auxiliary jack for connecting your music devices. And with six standard airbags as well as side-curtain airbags, you can rest easy knowing your family is safe on the road.
