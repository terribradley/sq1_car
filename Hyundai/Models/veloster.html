Test Drive the 2016 Veloster at Portland's top Hyundai Dealership

The first thing you'll notice about the Hyundai Veloster is its sleek, sporty build. The next thing you'll notice is the innovative three-door design. While most cars like it require passengers to crawl behind the seats to get in, the Veloster's third door allows for easy an entrance and exit for backseat passengers. Meanwhile, the pop-open rear hatch has plenty of space for luggage or equipment.

The Veloster is an enthusiastic performer, with a 201 horsepower engine and stylish 18 inch alloy wheels. Impress your friends with the 450-watt Dimension audio system, steering wheel mounted audio, and a 7 inch multimedia touch screen. Vehicle safety and stability is enhanced with the torque vectoring control, six standard airbags, and brake assist technology. What are you waiting for?
