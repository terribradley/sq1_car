Test Drive the 2016 Elantra GT at Portland's top Hyundai Dealership

The Hyundai Elantra GT features a sleek and sporty exterior with a European hatchback design. The GT provides the highest cargo volume in its class and offers several advanced technology amenities for a truly luxurious ride. The GT includes a cooled glove compartment, a touchscreen multimedia panel, driver selectable steering modes, a hidden rearview camera, and a six speed manual transmission. The Elantra GT offers economical performance, averaging 34 MPG on the highway with its 173 horsepower engine.
